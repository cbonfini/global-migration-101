Climate Change and Future Displacement

Faculty teaching handout · Skill emphasis: Systems Thinking

Explores how environmental change and climate impacts influence migration patterns, including slow- vs rapid-onset events, legal gaps, environmental justice, and interaction with economic migration.

Undergraduate outcomes

  1. Explain environmental drivers of migration
  2. Identify regions vulnerable to climate displacement
  3. Describe climate migration concepts and legal gaps
  4. Recognize the global impact and policy challenges

Graduate outcomes

  1. Evaluate policy frameworks addressing climate migration
  2. Model future climate migration scenarios
  3. Analyze links between climate vulnerability and displacement

Key topics & scope

Climate displacementslow vs rapid onset eventslegal gapsenvironmental justiceinteraction with economic migrationregions vulnerable to climate displacementpolicy challenges

Signature learning experience

Scenario planning exercise
