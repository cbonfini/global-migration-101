Global Migration 101 · Narratives & Ethics

Media, Narratives, and Representation

Faculty teaching handout · Skill emphasis: Analytical + Critical Thinking

Examines how migration is represented in media and public discourse, including framing, stereotypes, political narratives, misinformation, and the link between media and policy outcomes.

Undergraduate outcomes

  1. Identify common migration narratives and media bias
  2. Explain media influence on public perception
  3. Analyze migration coverage examples and compare sources
  4. Recognize misinformation and stereotypes

Graduate outcomes

  1. Critically analyze media framing of migration
  2. Critique narrative construction
  3. Evaluate the impact of narratives on policy and public opinion

Key topics & scope

Media framingstereotypespolitical narrativesmisinformationlink between media and policy outcomespublic perceptioncomparing sources

Signature learning experience
